# ai-services/

AI-Assisted Enterprise Intelligence Layer — kept as a distinct, independently auditable boundary so AI capabilities remain governed and human-reviewed at every stage.

See Section 13 (Three-Layer Architecture, Layer 3) and Section 15 (AI-Assisted Enterprise Intelligence) of the root README.

AI in this platform is AI-assisted, not AI-driven: every capability here augments human decision-makers and preserves human authority and accountability. Under Section 25 (Project Roadmap), this layer is introduced internally in Phase 2, under full human review, before any citizen-facing use.
